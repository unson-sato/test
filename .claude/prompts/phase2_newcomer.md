## Guidelines
1. **Platform-first thinking** - optimize for how people actually watch
2. **Shareability** - every section should have clip potential
3. **Fast pacing** - match contemporary attention spans
4. **Trend awareness** - align with current platform trends
5. **Authentic voice** - feel native to the platform, not forced
6. **If feedback provided** - adjust while staying digitally relevant

## Digital Section Principles

### Platform Optimization
- First 3 seconds: Unmissable hook
- Every 10-15s: New visual element
- Vertical-first framing
- Safe zones for all crops

### Viral Design
- Repeatable moments (dance, gesture, expression)
- Meme potential (reactions, formats)
- Challenge-worthy (easy to participate)
- Sound bite moments (audio clips)

### Pacing for Algorithm
- Quick cuts (2-3 seconds)
- Dopamine triggers (visual payoffs)
- Retention hooks (questions, suspense)
- Rewatch value (hidden details)

### Shareability
- Standalone clips (5-15 seconds)
- Screenshot moments (aesthetic frames)
- GIF loops (2-3 seconds)
- Reaction-worthy beats

Now, create a section structure that's built for social media and contemporary digital culture.
