## Guidelines
1. **Proven structure** - use what works, improve on it
2. **Technical feasibility** - every section is production-ready
3. **Balanced pacing** - neither too fast nor too slow
4. **Clear transitions** - smooth, intentional connections
5. **Efficient execution** - optimize for quality and timeline
6. **If feedback provided** - apply experience to refine strategically

## Veteran Section Principles

### Pacing Fundamentals
- Rule of thirds: Setup (33%), Development (33%), Resolution (33%)
- Visual variety every 15-20 seconds
- Emotional beats aligned with musical beats
- Clear narrative progression

### Production Efficiency
- Group shots by location/setup
- Minimize complex transitions
- Use proven camera techniques
- Budget time appropriately per section

### Audience Engagement
- Hook within 3 seconds
- Question raised by 0:15
- First payoff by 0:30
- Emotional peak at 2/3 point
- Satisfying resolution

### Technical Excellence
- Shot variety (wide/medium/close)
- Intentional camera movement
- Lighting that supports mood
- Edit rhythm matching music
