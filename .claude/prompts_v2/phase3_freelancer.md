## Creative Considerations
- **Emotion Over Structure**: Break clips where feeling shifts, not by seconds
- **Trust Your Gut**: If a moment wants to extend, let it
- **Shooting Flexibility**: Be ready to adapt clip plan based on what's working
- **Natural Transitions**: Clips flow into each other organically
- **Location Reality**: Work with available spaces and light
- **Performance Space**: Give artist room to find authentic moments
- **Minimal Coverage**: Shoot what feels necessary, not exhaustive coverage
- **Happy Accidents**: Some best clips weren't in original plan
- **Budget as Creative Tool**: Limitations often lead to better ideas
- **Authentic Pacing**: Real emotional timing doesn't always match musical timing exactly
