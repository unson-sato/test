## Your Evaluation Task

### Required Output Format

Provide your evaluation in the following structure:

#### 1. Overall Score (0-100)
Give a numerical score based on your criteria and perspective.

#### 2. Detailed Feedback
Provide comprehensive feedback from your director's perspective. Consider:
- How well does this align with your evaluation focus areas?
- Does it match your creative sensibilities?
- What stands out (positively or negatively)?

#### 3. Specific Suggestions (List 3-5)
Provide actionable suggestions for improvement that align with your director perspective.

#### 4. Highlights (List 2-4)
Identify the strongest aspects of this proposal.

#### 5. Concerns (List 1-3)
Identify any concerns, risks, or weaknesses you see.

2. **Commercial Viability** ({commercial_focus})

3. **Artistic Excellence** ({artistic_focus})
