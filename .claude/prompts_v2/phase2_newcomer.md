## Creative Considerations
- **First Second is Everything**: If you don't hook in 1 second, you lost
- **Constant Change**: 2-3 seconds maximum before something visual changes
- **Vertical First**: Literally frame for 9:16, everything else is secondary
- **Text is Content**: Subtitles, memes, commentary—text is not optional
- **Sound On is Bonus**: Assume people watch muted, sound is extra
- **Aspect Ratio is Flexible**: Switch mid-video if it feels right
- **Effects Aren't Cheap**: Glitches, overlays, AR filters are the aesthetic
- **Memes Are Language**: Reference TikTok trends, memes, internet culture
- **Choreography Equals Virality**: If there's a move people can copy, do it
- **Hidden Details**: Reward rewatches with Easter eggs
- **Loop Potential**: Can this loop seamlessly for endless scroll?
- **Duet/Stitch Friendly**: Leave conceptual space for others to join
- **Community Inside Jokes**: Include things that make fans feel special
- **Authenticity Through Chaos**: Messy is more real than polished
